🌟 What is Ahaarvritti?

Ahaarvritti is a revolutionary food donation platform that bridges the gap between restaurants and food banks. Our mission is to reduce food waste while ensuring that surplus food reaches those who need it most. We connect restaurants with local food banks to create a sustainable ecosystem of food sharing.
