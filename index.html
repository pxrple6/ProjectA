Fighting Malnutrition One Meal at a Time

Annadatri is a student initiative, committed to ending malnutrition through sustainable food programs, education, and community empowerment initiatives.

Get Started ▶ How it Works

We fight malnutrition with your support!

Projet Annadatri was founded in 2025, Anusha took the lead to start the effort, tackling nutrition challenges across all age groups in vulnerable communities.

Our approach combines immediate food aid with long-term sustainability programs, nutrition education, and community empowerment initiatives that create lasting change.

Meet The Founder

Turning concern into action, one meal at a time.

Anusha

"Hi, I'm Anusha — the founder of Annadatri. I started this initiative because I couldn't ignore the hunger I saw around me every day. It broke my heart to see food wasted while so many went without a meal. Annadatri is my way of doing something about it — of turning concern into action."

Recognition

Milestones that mark our journey from a local idea to a global stage

2024 · Origin

Founding Annadatri

Launched Annadatri, a student-led initiative to bridge the gap between food waste and malnutrition — the foundation of a zero-hunger mission.

🌱 Student Initiative
2024 · Tech

Ahaarvritti — Top 40, Samsung Solve for Tomorrow

Developed Ahaarvritti, a web app connecting donors with excess food directly to NGOs in real-time. Recognised in the Top 40 of Samsung Solve for Tomorrow (SFT) at IIT Delhi.

🏆 Top 40 · IIT Delhi
2024 · Global

Top 50 Changemakers · United Nations, Geneva

Represented at the 9th 1M1B Active Impact Summit at the United Nations, Geneva. Recognised as one of the World's Top 50 Changemakers in the Changemakers World Cup by 1M1B — felicitated at the UN headquarters in Geneva, Switzerland.

🌍 Top 50 Worldwide · UN Geneva
