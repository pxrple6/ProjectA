Annadatri is a student initiative, committed to ending malnutrition through sustainable food programs, education, and community empowerment initiatives.

Meet The Founder

Turning concern into action, one meal at a time.

Anusha

“Hi, I’m Anusha — the founder of Annadatri. I started this initiative because I couldn’t ignore the hunger I saw around me every day. It broke my heart to see food wasted while so many went without a meal. Annadatri is my way of doing something about it — of turning concern into action. From coordinating donations to building a team of passionate changemakers, I’m here to make sure every plate counts.”
